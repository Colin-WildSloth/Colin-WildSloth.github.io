About Me

My name is Colin Wild, I am a student at Maricopa Community College creating this website as my final project for my Internet/Web Development class. I created this website as a place where people can gather and post their recipes, whether it is a grandmas’ original or something you produced on a tight budget, all are welcome here. Ever sine my days in the Boy Scouts of America, I have enjoyed cooking, from using what little ingredients we have for dinner at camp to following a recipe I found online to cook for dinner that night.
